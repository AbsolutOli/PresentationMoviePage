about

The James Bond series focuses on a fictional Britith Secret Servic agent created in 1953 by writer Ian Fleming who featured him in twelve novels and two short-story collection. Scince Fleming’s death in1964, eight other autors have written authorised Bond hovels or novelizations: Fingsley Amis, Christopher Wood John Gardner, Raymond Benson. sebastian Faulks, Jeffery Deaver, William Boyd and Anthony Horowitz. The latest novel is Forever and a Day by Anthony Horowitz, published in May 2018.
